AVAILABLE FEBRUARY 25, 2025

(PRE-ORDER DECEMBER 17, 2024)

Brace yourself for the raw intensity of Fusers debut album, Storm. This collection of tracks is a powerful journey through the turbulence of life, blending gritty rock and grunge elements with deeply personal storytelling. From the confrontational anthem Wanted To to the nostalgic yearning of Memorias Veteris Domus, each track delves into themes of loss, resilience, and the relentless pursuit of meaning.

Feel the searing emotion in Gone But Forgotten and the fierce tribute in The Blinds, a track dedicated to a friend lost in a tragic car crash. Resentment veils a simmering rage against unseen forces that strip away everything, while Plea captures the raw struggle of battling inner demons. Pandora reflects on the bittersweet memories of youth and the unsettling question of what might have been, wrapped in the rough edges of lifes realities.

With intense riffs, rugged vocals, and unflinching lyrics, Storm captures the essence of lifes darkest moments and the strength found in their wake. Let Fusers Storm take you on a ride through the chaos and emerge stronger on the other side.
