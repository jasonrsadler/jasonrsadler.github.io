AVAILABLE FEBRUARY 25, 2025

(PRE-ORDER DECEMBER 17, 2024)

Brace yourself for the raw intensity of Fuser’s debut album, “Storm.” This collection of tracks is a powerful journey through the turbulence of life, blending gritty rock and grunge elements with deeply personal storytelling. From the confrontational anthem “Wanted To” to the nostalgic yearning of “Memorias Veteris Domus,” each track delves into themes of loss, resilience, and the relentless pursuit of meaning.

Feel the searing emotion in “Gone But Forgotten” and the fierce tribute in “The Blinds,” a track dedicated to a friend lost in a tragic car crash. “Resentment” veils a simmering rage against unseen forces that strip away everything, while “Plea” captures the raw struggle of battling inner demons. “Pandora” reflects on the bittersweet memories of youth and the unsettling question of what might have been, wrapped in the rough edges of life’s realities.

With intense riffs, rugged vocals, and unflinching lyrics, “Storm” captures the essence of life’s darkest moments and the strength found in their wake. Let Fuser’s “Storm” take you on a ride through the chaos and emerge stronger on the other side.

AVAILABLE NOVEMBER 12, 2024.

Get ready to feel the raw power of Fuser’s latest single, “The Blinds.” This gritty track is a fierce tribute to a friend lost in a tragic car crash over two decades ago. With intense riffs and rugged vocals, Fuser channels the turmoil and resilience that come with such a profound loss. “The Blinds” offers a glimpse into the upcoming album “Storm,” promising a journey through life’s darkest moments and the strength found in the aftermath. Let the music take you through the storm and emerge stronger on the other side.
